Laboratory 9 - exemple

A string of unsigned numbers represented on 16 bits are given. Print on the screen all the numbers from the string.
  • Main module - lab9-pp-en.asm
  • Secondary module - lab9-tipar-en.asm
In order to obtain the final executable file:

tasm /zi lab9-pp-en
tasm /zi lab9-tipar-en
tlink /v lab9-pp-en lab9-tipar-en
