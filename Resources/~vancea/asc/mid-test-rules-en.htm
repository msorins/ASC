Important information regarding mid term practical test on 10th December 2016

Students will be able to access the mid term practical test by using their username and password created on the platform http://moodle.cs.ubbcluj.ro/. All students are required to create an account before the mid term practical test.
In order to create a new account and to access the quizes you have to follow the steps:
  1. Moodle is available at http://moodle.cs.ubbcluj.ro.
  2. At your first visit you will need to create a New Account using your user ID as Username (ex: xyie1234) and your SCS email address (ex. xyie1234@scs.ubbcluj.ro).
  3. An email confirmation will be sent to your SCS email address which can be accessed via: https://www.scs.ubbcluj.ro/webmail.
  4. The next step is editing your profile, where you have to upload a recent profile picture.
  5. Once logged in you need to enrol yourself into the ASC course and start practicing the quizzes.
You can watch a video tutorial about creating a new account on Moodle: or the enrolment tutorial on a Moodle course:
Students can participate to the mid term practical test based on a schedule which will be published on Friday, 9th of December 2016.
Every student needs the ID card, the student identity card and he/she must have the Moodle account active and with an updated profile picture.

Any problems regarding creating Moodle accounts must be sent via email address: radu.dragos@cs.ubbcluj.ro

Three quizzes are available on Moodle:
  1. Quiz Numeral Systems
  2. Quiz Basic Operations
  3. Quiz Strings
Feel free to practice on it.
Starting with 5th December 2016 a sample test will be available on Moodle which will have a time limit of 30 minutes. Every student is required to solve the sample test.
